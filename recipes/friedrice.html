Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a personal favorite of mine, and this recipe is one that I use often when I am hungry and want something easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Chile Flakes
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seasame Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Frozen Veggies
  • Preffered Rice

Steps

  1. Dice chicken and place in a mixing pot, add seasonings (except sugar), minced garlic, soy sauce, and seasame oil and let it marinate for about 15-30 min.
  2. Start rice and steam veggies while the meat marinates (I have a 2-in-1 rice cooker and steamer so it saves time).
  3. Cook chicken on medium heat for about 6-8 minutes
  4. Once rice and veggies are finished add to pot
  5. Add a bit of soy sauce for color and a pinch of sugar then mix well and serve!
